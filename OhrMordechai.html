Our Sacred Journey

For fifteen blessed years, Congregation Ohr Mordechai has been the beating heart of our Orthodox community. What began as a humble gathering in a small basement has blossomed into something truly extraordinary.

Last year marked a momentous milestone in our community's history as we moved into our magnificent new building. This transition represents not just a change of location, but the fulfillment of years of dreams, prayers, and collective dedication.

Our new home provides us with the space we've always envisioned - room for our entire community to come together in prayer, study, and celebration. Every corner of our building has been designed with kedushah (holiness) in mind, creating an environment where spiritual growth and community connection flourish naturally.
